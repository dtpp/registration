Vietnam Pilot Supplier Development Program

The Pilot Supplier Development Program supports domestic suppliers throughout the value chain in targeted sectors. It helps domestic suppliers set up their businesses to meet the demands of multinational enterprises, which include quality, price, and delivery, among others. Once they have shown that they can fulfill these and other requirements, then they are linked to the multinationals for future supply opportunities.
